Hello, and welcome to my website. I am Gyeongmin Kim (GM Kim), a researcher with an M.S. in [Intelligent Robotics](https://robot.skku.edu/robot_en/index.do){:target="_blank"} from Sungkyunkwan University, where I worked in the [Helper Lab](https://hlab.skku.edu/){:target="_blank"} under the supervision of Professor [Mun-Taek Choi](https://robot.skku.edu/robot_en/faculty.do?mode=view&perId=LZStrBwOQlgzg6gdgkgDwIYCkDCAhATAUzABhgHEBOHALQEEBeaoA%20&){:target="_blank"}. I also hold a B.S. in [Mechanical Engineering](https://mech.skku.edu/me_eng/index.do){:target="_blank"} from the same university. 

My primary research interests focus on:
- Dynamic System Control
- Data Analysis
- Data-driven Control
- Physics-guided Learning

Through various research projects and competitions, I have built a strong foundation in both mechanical design and software algorithms. I am highly motivated to continuously advance my knowledge and practical skills, particularly in the intersection of physics and data-driven methodologies.
